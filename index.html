Elizabeth is a junior at the University of Pennsylvania from San Bruno, California double majoring in Classics and Cognitive Science. As an undergraduate research assistant for projects at Penn and Stanford, she is passionate about the intersection of computation and archaeology. Working as an RA at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia (CHOP) Elizabeth works to promote safety and awareness regarding emerging technologies (AI, ML, autonomous vehicles). As head TA for CIS 105 (Python for Data Analytics) and Chair of Outreach on the Penn Classics Undergraduate Advisory Board (UAB), Elizabeth is passionate about STEM and humanities education and outreach. Outside of academia, Elizabeth is an editor at Discentes, Penn's undergraduate Classics journal, and is the principal harpist of the Penn Symphony Orchestra.
